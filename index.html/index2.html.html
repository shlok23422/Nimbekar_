Rules and Regulations

  1. We are aware that regularity for the classes is a must failing which student may be expelled from the academy.
  2. Student must maintain the discipline of the academy.
  3. Student must appear for all the performance evaluation test.
  4. Fees must be paid as per the schedule; otherwise student will not be allowed to attend the classes and Rs. 50 per day will be fined.
  5. Parents must attend the meetings called by the academy to discuss the progress of the student.
  6. Fees once paid will not be refunded under any circumstances.
  7. Timings of the classes will be intimated to the students from time to time which they must follow.
  8. Rules and Regulations of the Academy as informed to the students from time to time will be applicable to all the students.
  9. Use of mobile phones in the classes and reading room is strictly prohibited.
  10. I have no objection to my name and photograph being used for the advertisements of the academy.

I have read the rules and I undertake to abide by them.
